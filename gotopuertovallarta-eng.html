Go to Puerto Vallarta

Role
Web design and fontend developer
Project
Multilingual Website
Technologies
Wordpress, Web Standards, Angular.

Go to Puerto Vallarta is a collection of luxury condos, strategically located in the romantic area of old Puerto Vallarta and offering an LGBT experience.

The best condos in old Vallarta.

The project started as an informative website in WordPress since the client wanted to manage a blog and focus on SEO positioning. However, as the requirements grew, the project evolved into a custom-built solution that also enabled direct condominium rentals, reducing commission costs.

The result was a hybrid website, which involved developing the informative part of the site in Wordpress, allowing the client to self-manage content without touching the code. The condos list, availability schedules, booking user flow, and payment process were built with the ability to connect via API's with external property managers centralizing information and preventing overselling.

I carried out the redesign of the visual identity and brand renown to align the product with strategic objectives. To make the experience even more memorable, I directed a video tour that shows the most attractive areas of Puerto Vallarta when loading the site. This was repeated in the condominiums section, giving more details about the dimensions and amenities offered by each option, with the aim of encouraging rent.
